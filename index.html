Get to Know Us

Welcome to THE THRONE ROOM

Step into a sacred atmosphere of worship and divine fellowship. The Throne Room is a ministry devoted to deepening intimacy with God through praise, worship, and the leading of the Holy Spirit—equipping believers to live in constant awareness of His glorious presence.

Discover more

Our Programs

Spiritual Growth & Fellowship Activities

Created for Fellowship with God

A weekly Wednesday gathering devoted to strengthening our communion with God through praise, worship, and the Word in true fellowship.

Children’s Prayer

Every Monday, our young ones gather joyfully to pray, sing, and grow in the love and wisdom of Jesus Christ through guided teaching.

The Crowns of Jesus

Our annual Easter celebration commemorating Christ’s victory through powerful worship, drama, and heartfelt expressions of gratitude.

The Edge of Fasting

A yearly fasting and prayer encounter held from December 1–21, seeking renewal, strength, and divine direction for the coming year.

The Christ in Christmas

A joyful December event celebrating the true reason for the season—Christ Himself—through songs, drama, and messages of hope.

News and Upcoming Events

Stay informed on weekly programs, outreach activities, and revival meetings. Download event fliers and join us in worship and fellowship.
